Statistical Data Analysis · TripleTen

Megaline Prepaid Plan Revenue Analysis

Analyzed usage and revenue for a telecom operator with two prepaid plans (Surf and Ultimate) using data from 500 clients. Cleaned and prepared call, message, internet, and user datasets; calculated monthly usage and revenue per customer; compared customer behavior with descriptive statistics and histograms; and tested hypotheses about revenue differences between plans and between the NY–NJ region and other areas.

  • Prepared messy real-world data and applied Megaline’s rounding rules for minutes and data
  • Built monthly revenue metrics from plan fees and overage charges
  • Compared Surf vs Ultimate behavior using mean, variance, standard deviation, and distributions
  • Ran hypothesis tests to support advertising-budget recommendations

Exploratory Data Analysis · TripleTen

Instacart Customer Shopping Habits

Cleaned and explored a modified Instacart grocery-delivery dataset to report on how customers shop. Worked across five related tables (orders, products, order items, aisles, and departments), fixed nonstandard CSV formatting, handled missing and duplicate values, and built visualizations that show when people order, how often they reorder, and which products are most popular.

  • Preprocessed multi-table data: fixed types, filled/removed missing values, and removed duplicates
  • Mapped order patterns by hour of day, day of week, and days between orders
  • Compared Wednesday vs Saturday order-hour distributions and customer order-frequency patterns
  • Identified top products, top reorders, cart-size behavior, and items most often added first
